## Publishing steps

For each post:

1. Go to https://dev.to/new
2. Paste the body
3. In the "Canonical URL" field, set: `https://signupdoggy.pages.dev/blog/<slug>`
4. Add the tags
5. Publish
6. Wait 24 hours for Google to crawl the dev.to URL and pass link equity

**Important**: dev.to requires all posts to have a canonical URL pointing to the original source to avoid duplicate content. We're using that requirement to our advantage.
